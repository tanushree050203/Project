MARKETING MANAGEMENT

Marketing management after 12th involves pursuing courses and programs in marketing after completing high school. These courses are available at certificate, diploma, and undergraduate levels and can be pursued through online, offline, or distance learning modes. The curriculum covers various marketing strategies, practices, and new techniques, focusing on areas such as advertising, segmentation, positioning, sales, and relations. After completing these courses, individuals can pursue careers as marketing managers, brand managers, market research analysts, and more, with average salaries ranging from INR 6 - 12 LPA, which can increase with experience.
