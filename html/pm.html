PROJECT MANAGEMENT

Project management after 12th offers various courses such as Certificate in Project Management, MBA Project Management, and Postgraduate Diploma in Project Management. These courses focus on providing relevant skills, theoretical knowledge, and tools to set project goals. Students from any discipline, preferably commerce, can pursue these courses after completing 12th grade.
Eligibility criteria: The eligibility criteria for different project management courses vary, and the courses can be pursued as full-time, part-time, or online.
Skills: Skills such as teamwork, leadership, and communication are essential for a career as a project manager.
Job opportunities: Project management courses are in high demand nationally and internationally, offering better job opportunities and higher salaries for advancement.
