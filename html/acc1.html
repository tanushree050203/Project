ACCOUNTANT

"Accountant after 12th" provides comprehensive information about accounting courses available after completing 12th grade. It covers various levels of courses such as certificate, diploma, bachelor's, master's, and doctorate in accounting. It also discusses the eligibility criteria, career scope, skills required, fees, and top colleges offering these courses. Additionally, it outlines the job profiles and average salaries for professionals in the accounting field. Overall, it serves as a valuable guide for individuals interested in pursuing a career in accounting after completing 12th grade.
Eligibility criteria: The eligibility criteria for accounting courses after 12th typically require candidates to have completed their 12th standard education with a focus on commerce subjects such as accounts, mathematics, and economics. The admission process is usually based on counseling after an entrance exam, and the course fees can range from INR 1,00,000 to 4,00,000 per annum, depending on the institution and the specific course.
Career scope : In terms of career scope, accounting offers a wide range of opportunities, not limited to commerce students. Students from science and arts backgrounds can also pursue careers in accounting and finance. The skills required for accounting courses include commercial and business awareness, excellent communication and presentation skills, an analytical approach to work, high numeracy, and sound technical skills.
Fees: The fees for accounting courses can vary significantly depending on the specific course and the institution. For example, the Diploma in Taxation at Symbiosis International University and Guru Nanak Dev University ranges from INR 14,500 to 1,00,000. Similarly, the Diploma in Accounting and Auditing at Arcade Business College has a fee range of INR 14,500 to 1,00,000.
Top colleges: Top colleges offering accounting courses include Goenka World Institute Gurgaon, IILM Undergraduate Business School New Delhi, Indian School of Management and Entrepreneurship Mumbai, Presidency College Bangalore, and WLC College Chennai for B.Sc. Accounting, with average fees ranging from INR 2,000 to 20,00,00. For M.Com Accountancy, colleges like IGNOU and Presidency University are popular choices.
Skills: The skills required for accounting courses include commercial and business awareness, excellent communication and presentation skills, an analytical approach to work, high numeracy, and sound technical skills.
Duration: The duration of accounting courses can vary based on the level of study and the specific program. Typically, the duration of accounting courses is as follows:

  • Certificate Programs: 3 months to 1 year
  • Bachelor’s Degree (B.Com, BBA, etc.): 3 to 4 years
  • Postgraduate Courses (M.Com, MBA, etc.): 2 years
  • Certified Management Accountant (CMA): 6 months to 4 years
  • Chartered Certified Accountant (ACCA): 6 months to 2 years

    • These durations are approximate and may vary based on the specific institution and program requirements.
    Entrance exam: For admissions in bachelor accounting courses, students can gain entry through entrance exams as well as merit lists. Some of the top universities providing undergraduate accounting courses entrance exams include ICWAI, ICMI, and more. Additionally, for postgraduate courses such as M.Com and MBA, universities may conduct their own entrance exams for admissions.
    Salary: The average salary for individuals starting their career in accounting after completing a certificate course is around INR 2 – 3 LPA (lakhs per annum). However, the salary range can vary based on factors such as the specific role, level of education, and the employer.
    “Those candidates who want to start their career in this field with a certificate course can get an average salary of INR 2 – 3 LPA only in the beginning.”
