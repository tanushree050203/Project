INTERIOR DESIGN

Interior design is a popular vocational subject offered by top arts and design universities in India. Students can pursue various courses such as Diploma in Interior Designing, undergraduate and postgraduate degrees in Interior Design, as well as certificate courses in Interior Design and related specializations. Some popular courses after 12th include BDes Interior Design, BA/BSc Interior Design, and Diploma in Interior Designing. Postgraduate programs like MDes Interior Design, MA/MSc Interior Design, or Postgraduate Diploma in Interior Design are available after completing an undergraduate degree in Interior Design courses.
The courses cover a wide range of subjects such as Design Elements and Process, Auto CAD, Flat rendering and layouts, Colour and Visualizations, Decor and Planning, History of Crafts & Interior Design, Technical Drawing, Computer applications, Building Services, Anthropometry and Ergonomics, Retail Display & Design, Lighting Design Fundamentals, and many more. Students are also required to possess skills such as Adobe Photoshop, AutoCAD, Schematic Designs, Creative Mindset, Communication Skills, Microsoft Office, Drawing and Sketching, and 3D Rendering.
Interior Designing courses are popular among students interested in pursuing a career in the field of design. The demand for interior designers is on the rise, and it offers a rewarding career that entails creating functional and aesthetically pleasing spaces for clients. Interior designers can find opportunities in various areas such as residential design, commercial design, furniture arrangement, healthcare design, home lighting advising, and more.
The average starting salary in the interior designing field is around INR 3,00,000 - 5,00,000, and experienced interior designers tend to earn more due to their expertise and skills. The top recruiters for interior design professionals include companies such as Inertia, Tag Concepts, Acropolis, Urban Ladder, and Livspace.
