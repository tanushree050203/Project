PHARMACY

"Pharmacy Courses after 10th." The document outlines various pharmacy courses available to students after completing 10th grade. It discusses the eligibility criteria, top colleges, career scope, and the list of available courses. Pharmacy courses after 10th prepare students for entry-level positions as pharmacy assistants or technicians. The most sought-after courses include Diploma in Pharmacy Assistant, Certificate in Veterinary Pharmacy, Certificate in Pharmacy Assistant, and Certificate in Pharmaceutical Management. These courses equip students with the necessary skills to work in the pharmaceutical industry, healthcare sector, and patient care. It also highlights the eligibility criteria and career prospects for students pursuing pharmacy courses after 10th.
ELIGIBILITY CRITERIA : The eligibility criteria for students pursuing pharmacy courses after 10th typically require a minimum of 50% marks in science and mathematics. However, eligibility requirements may vary by institution and program. Some schools may also require students to have completed a pre- pharmacy course or equivalent degree. It's advisable to verify the specific qualifying requirements of the institution and course before applying for pharmacy courses after 10th.
CAREER PROSPECTS: As for career prospects, students who pursue pharmacy courses after 10th have a wide range of employment options. Entry- level positions for pharmacy graduates include roles such as pharmacy technician, retail pharmacist assistant, hospital pharmacy assistant, clinical research coordinator, and medical representative. The average salaries for these roles range from 1 to 5 lakhs INR annually, depending on the specific job role and experience.
