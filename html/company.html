COMPANY SECRETARY

Becoming a company secretary (CS) after 12th typically involves the following steps:

  • Eligibility Criteria: To pursue CS after 12th, you must pass the 10+2 examination from a recognized board or equivalent. There are no specific subject requirements at the 10+2 level for pursuing CS.
  • Duration of Course: The CS course is divided into three stages: Foundation, Executive, and Professional. The duration varies depending on the level. After 12th, you would start with the Foundation Program, which usually takes around 8-9 months to complete.
  • Career Prospects: CS professionals have various career opportunities in both the public and private sectors. They can work as company secretaries, corporate legal advisors, corporate planners, governance professionals, etc. CS also opens up opportunities for consultancy and entrepreneurship.
  • Salary: Salaries for company secretaries vary depending on factors like location, sector, experience, and the size of the organization. However, CS professionals generally earn competitive salaries, with the potential for growth as they gain experience and expertise.
  • Entrance Exams: There are no specific entrance exams for CS after 12th. However, you would need to clear the Foundation Program examination conducted by the Institute of Company Secretaries of India (ICSI) to move on to the Executive Program.
  • Course Fees: The course fees for the CS program can vary. It's advisable to check with the ICSI or respective coaching institutes for the most current fee structure.
Pursuing a career as a company secretary after 12th requires dedication, as the course involves rigorous training and examinations. However, it offers promising career prospects and opportunities for growth in the corporate world.
