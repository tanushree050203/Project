PHARMACY

"Pharmacy Courses after 12th Science: Eligibility, Scope, Fees" provides an extensive overview of pharmacy courses after 12th science. It covers various courses such as B.Pharm, B.Pharm (Hons.), B.Pharm (Lateral), B.Pharm Ayurveda, M.Pharm, and D.Pharm. It also discusses the eligibility criteria, entrance exams, best colleges in India, scope, careers, and pharmacist salaries in India. It outlines the details of each course, including the duration, examination type, eligibility, admission process, and employment sectors.
Additionally, it highlights the specialization courses in pharmacy, entrance exams, and job opportunities in the pharmaceutical sector.
"Pharmacy Courses after 12th Science:

  • "B.Pharm, B.Pharm (Hons.), B.Pharm (Lateral), B.Pharm ... ... Ayurveda, M.Pharm, and D.Pharm are among the top Pharmacy courses after 12th Science."
  • "B.Pharm (Bachelor of Pharmacy)"
  • "D.Pharm (Diploma in Pharmacy)"
  • "M.Pharm (Masters in Pharmacy)"

    • Eligibility Criteria:
  • For B.Pharm: Completion of 10+2 with a minimum of 50% aggregate in biology and chemistry.
  • For B.Pharm (Lateral Entry): Completion of a Diploma in Pharmacy or Diploma in Biochemistry with a minimum of 50% aggregate.
  • For B.Pharm (Hons.): Completion of 10+2 with a minimum of 50% aggregate in biology and chemistry.
  • For M.Pharm: Completion of B.Pharm with not less than 50%.
  • For D.Pharm: Minimum eligibility qualification is 10+2 with Physics and Chemistry as compulsory subjects along with Mathematics or Biology.

    • Scope:
  • Graduates of pharmacy courses can work in various sectors such as biotechnology, biomedical research, medicine technology, hospitals, pharmacology, testing labs, and more.
  • There are job opportunities in pharmaceutical manufacturing, regulatory bodies, hospitals, clinical pharmacy, research agencies, educational institutes, and sales and marketing departments.

    • Fees: The course fees for pharmacy courses range from INR 9,000 to INR 2 lakh per annum, depending on the specific course and institution.
