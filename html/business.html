BUSINESS COURSES

It seems like you're interested in pursuing a business-related course after completing 10th grade. Typically, you can opt for diploma courses in fields like business administration, entrepreneurship, or retail management.

  • Eligibility criteria: Usually, the minimum requirement is passing the 10th-grade examination from a recognized board.
  • Duration: Diploma courses can range from 6 months to 2 years, depending on the program and institution.
  • Career prospects: With a diploma in business, you can explore entry-level positions in various industries such as marketing, sales, retail, customer service, or administration. As you gain experience and further qualifications, you can advance to managerial roles.
  • Salary: Entry-level salaries vary depending on factors like location, industry, and job role. However, they typically range from $20,000 to $40,000 annually.
  • Entrance: Entrance to diploma courses generally does not require any specific entrance exams after the 10th grade. Admission is usually based on merit or first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Course fees: Fees can vary significantly depending on the institution and the course duration. It's best to check with specific colleges or training centers for accurate fee information.
