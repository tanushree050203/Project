ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCE

Environmental science offers various courses in India after completing 10th and 12th grades. These include certificate courses, diploma courses, undergraduate (UG) courses, postgraduate (PG) courses, and PhD courses. The courses cover topics such as environmental issues, pollution, natural resources, energy systems, and sustainable development. Students can pursue these courses in various disciplines and across different institutions, including online platforms like Udemy, Coursera, IGNOU, and Alison. The average salary after completing an undergraduate or postgraduate degree in environmental science is around INR 5,00,000 annually. Admission to these courses is based on merit, entrance exams, and educational qualifications. Additionally, there are online certificate courses available, which are less expensive and have a shorter duration compared to degree courses. These courses cover topics such as environmental management, ecology, and sustainability. After completing these courses, job opportunities include roles such as environmental engineers, environmental biologists, and environmental scientists.
