PHYSIOTHERAPY

It provides details about pursuing a Diploma in Physiotherapy after completing 12th grade. It is a 2-year paramedical course that focuses on physical medicine and rehabilitation. The course covers theoretical studies and hands-on practice sessions. After completing this diploma, students can pursue further studies such as a Master of Physiotherapy course, PG Diploma in Physiotherapy, or advanced PhD studies. The healthcare sector offers various job opportunities for physiotherapists in settings such as hospitals, health centers, private practices, sports clubs, and NGOs. It also outlines the eligibility criteria, admission process, and career prospects associated with physiotherapy after 12th. Additionally, it mentions the scope of physiotherapy, job profiles, and average salaries for certified students. The admission process typically involves merit-based selection or entrance exams. It also lists some colleges offering the Diploma in Physiotherapy and provides information about the average annual fees for the course.
Eligibility:

  • Students must qualify for the 10+2 exam and score a minimum of 50% marks. Some colleges ask for a minimum of 60% in 10+2.
  • They must have Biology or Life Sciences as one of their main subjects in 10+2.
  • To apply for a postgraduate Physiotherapy course, students must have a BPT or BSc Physiotherapy degree from a recognized institution.
  • Apart from this, they either need to score more than 50% in BPT or crack the entrance exam (if any).
  • NEET score is not required to get admission to Physiotherapy programs. However, some colleges like Government Medical College, Nagpur consider NEET scores to offer BPT admission.
  • For a PhD degree in Physiotherapy, students need to clear PhD entrance exams like CSIR UGC NET or UGC NET. Then they need to qualify for the group discussion & personal interview rounds.
  • Students can practice Physiotherapy professionally after they complete a bachelor’s or a master’s degree in Physiotherapy.
  • They can either practice general Physiotherapy or specialize in a particular domain during their master’s like Sports Physiotherapy, Neuro Physiotherapy, Orthopedic Physiotherapy, etc.
  • A general Physiotherapist earns INR 0.3 – 2.5 lakhs annually as a fresher. A master’s degree increases the chances of a salary hike.

The scope of physiotherapy, job profiles, and average salaries for certified students:- The scope of physiotherapy Is quite broad, with job opportunities available in various sectors. Physiotherapists can work in healthcare institutions, rehabilitation clinics, gyms, sports franchises, and even start their own physiotherapy clinics. They can also find employment in colleges, universities, defense medical organizations, orthopedic departments, the pharmaceutical industry, and both private and government sectors. Additionally, there is a growing demand for physiotherapists in the healthcare and hospitality industries, as well as in sports industries as rehab specialists for athletes.
    Some common job profiles for certified physiotherapy students include lecturer, research assistant, researcher, self-employed private physiotherapist, sports physio rehabilitator, therapy manager, and customer care assistant. The average starting salary for certified students ranges from INR 3,00,000 to 20,00,000 per annum, depending on the job profile and the employer.
