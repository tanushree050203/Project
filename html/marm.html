MARKETING MANAGEMENT

Marketing management is a popular field for students after 12th. Here's some information :-

  • Eligibility Criteria: Typically, students need to have completed their 12th grade with a certain percentage (varies by institution and country) to be eligible for admission into marketing management programs.
  • Duration: The duration of undergraduate programs in marketing management is usually three to four years, depending on the country and the specific curriculum.
  • Career Prospects: Graduates can pursue careers in various industries, including advertising, brand management, market research, public relations, sales, digital marketing, and more. Career prospects are diverse, with opportunities to work in both corporate and non-profit sectors.
  • Salary: Entry-level salaries for marketing management graduates vary by country, industry, and job role. However, they can range from moderate to lucrative, especially with experience and specialization.
  • Entrance Exams: Some institutions may require students to take entrance exams for admission into marketing management programs. These exams vary by region and institution. Examples include SAT, ACT, or specific entrance exams conducted by universities.
  • Course Fees: Course fees vary widely depending on the institution, country, and program level (undergraduate or postgraduate). It's essential to research specific institutions to get accurate information about fees and financial aid options.
Additionally, pursuing advanced degrees like an MBA with a focus on marketing can further enhance career opportunities and earning potential in marketing management.
