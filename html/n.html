    Salary: The salary for nursing professionals in India can vary based on factors such as experience, qualifications, and the specific role or specialization. Generally, a fresher in the nursing field can expect to earn between INR 3 to 7 lakhs annually. However, with experience and advanced qualifications, nurses can potentially earn higher salaries. For example, the average salary for a registered nurse in the government sector is around INR 3 to 4 lakhs per annum, while in prestigious institutions like AIIMS, the salary range is approximately INR 3.6 to 4.6 lakhs per annum. It’s important to note that these figures are approximate and can vary based on individual circumstances and employer policies.
